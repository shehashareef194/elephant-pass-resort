ABOUT

Elephant Pass Ayurveda & Yoga Retreat, the best Ayurveda resort in Kochi, offers authentic treatments in a serene riverside setting. Surrounded by lush mahogany forests and overlooking the Periyar River, the resort combines relaxation, rejuvenation, and wellness. With upgraded rooms, tranquil ambiance, and exceptional facilities, it provides a perfect blend of Ayurveda, yoga, and nature for a truly unforgettable experience.

We aim to provide an unforgettable and rejuvenating experience to our guests that will help them restore balance and harmony in their lives.
The Elephant Pass Ayurveda Resort has all the amenities you would expect from a world-class hotel, combined with the peace and tranquillity that can only be found in an ayurvedic resort. To provide the best Ayurveda and yoga retreat experience to our guests Elephant Pass Ayurveda and Yoga Retreat Resort strictly follows the government rules and regulations and is also certified under Ayush by Govt. of Kerala.
